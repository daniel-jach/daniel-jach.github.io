Et alia

onSET

Since October 2021, I am in charge of the onSET Test Center at Southwest Jiaotong University. The onSET is a worldwide language test in C-test format that provides a quick and accurate assessment of general language proficiency in English or German. The test measures levels between A2 and C1 of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages. Please contact me if you have any questions or would like to take the test.

Study in Germany

As a DAAD Lektor, I am responsible for providing information about studying in Germany. Please contact me to arrange a visit to your institution or download my presentation slides for more information.
